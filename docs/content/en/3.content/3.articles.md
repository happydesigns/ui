---
title: Articles
description: Use the article collection for blog posts, updates, and article-like content.
---

Use `article` when content should appear in a list, have a detail page, and carry article traits such as dates, categories, authors, status, and surrounding links.

## Typical setup

```vue
<template>
  <HArticleList collection="article" />
</template>
```

`HArticleList` renders category navigation and delegates the result grid to `HArticleGrid`. Use `HArticleGrid` directly when a page should show a filtered or simpler list without category tabs.

## Frontmatter patterns

Common article fields include `title`, `description`, `date`, `status`, `category`, and `authors`. Category display and author resolution come from app config and the `user` collection.

Keep article-specific display data in frontmatter. Keep list defaults, badge configuration, and repeated category labels in app config.
