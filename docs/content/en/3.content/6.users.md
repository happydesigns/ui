---
title: Users
description: Use the user collection for authors and people referenced from content.
---

Use `user` for author and person records that articles and events can reference. This keeps person data out of individual content files and allows article lists to resolve author display data consistently.

## Use users for

- Article authors.
- Event contacts.
- People displayed by reusable trait components.

## Keep local when

A person only appears once in prose and does not need structured rendering, link metadata, or reuse across content. In that case, normal Markdown text is enough.
