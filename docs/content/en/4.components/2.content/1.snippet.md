`HSnippet` renders one document from the `snippet` collection. Use it for reusable Markdown fragments that should stay content-owned but appear inside Vue templates or other content pages.

## Usage

::ui-example{:preview='false' name="snippet-basic"}
::

Snippets are useful for repeated editorial content such as contact blocks, legal fragments, opening hours, or short announcements. Keep the fragment in content when editors should own the text, and keep layout-specific composition in Vue when the surrounding structure belongs to the project.

```vue [components/ContactSection.vue]
<template>
  <section>
    <h2>Contact</h2>
    <HSnippet path="/snippets/contact" />
  </section>
</template>
```
