Variants describe reusable features that can be attached to content types. A page can have layout and table-of-contents behavior. An article can have dates, authors, categories, copy buttons, and surrounding links. An event can reuse most article behavior and add location or external links.

The registry lives in `nuxt.config.ts`; runtime defaults live in `app.config.ts`.

## How to use variants

Use a variant when a feature is part of a repeatable content type. For example, article-like collections can share category badges and list settings, while a one-off project page can stay local.

Do not use variants to hide product behavior or authorization rules. Variants are a presentation and content-structure tool. Domain rules belong in the product or app that owns the behavior.
